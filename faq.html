Who are we?

We are a small company based out of southeastern Washington with the goal of bringing quality custom miniatures to your table top games.

What services do we offer?

We offer custom 3-D printed miniatures, terrain, decoration items. These can be on a 1 inch square base, a 1x2 inch rectangular base, or a 4 inch square base.

How do we make our miniatures?

We make our miniatures using the latest in 3-D printing technology. For our metal miniatures, we create a mold from the printed plastic miniature.

What materials are available?

Currently we have a detailed plastic and a durable plastic. We are working on getting our forge up so that we can do metal printing.
